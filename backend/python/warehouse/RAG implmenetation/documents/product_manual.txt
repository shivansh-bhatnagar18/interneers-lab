Lego Castle Product Manual

The Lego Castle construction set by LEGO includes over 500 interlocking pieces used to build a medieval castle with towers, gates, and knight figures.

Recommended age: 8 years and above.

Warranty:
The Lego Castle comes with a 1 year manufacturer warranty covering missing or damaged pieces.

Assembly:
Assembly instructions are included inside the package. Adult supervision is recommended for younger children.

Safety:
Contains small parts. Not suitable for children under 3 years of age.


Lego City Set Product Manual

The Lego City Set allows children to construct roads, buildings, and emergency vehicles using modular construction bricks.

Recommended age: 7 years and above.

Warranty:
Includes a 1 year manufacturer warranty.

Maintenance:
Store pieces in a dry environment to avoid discoloration.


Wooden Blocks Product Manual

Wooden Blocks by FunWood are educational toys designed to improve creativity, coordination, and motor skills.

The blocks are made from non-toxic polished wood and are safe for toddlers above 2 years.

Cleaning:
Clean with a dry cloth only. Avoid water exposure.
