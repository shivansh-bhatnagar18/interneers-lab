Vendor FAQ

Q: How often should vendors update inventory?
A: Vendors should update stock quantities every week.

Q: What happens if delivery is delayed?
A: Repeated delayed deliveries may incur penalties or temporary suspension.

Q: Are educational toys reviewed differently?
A: Yes. Educational toys such as Wooden Blocks and Soft Building Blocks undergo additional safety checks.

Q: Which products have the highest demand?
A: Construction Toys and Educational Toys currently have the highest customer demand.

Q: What is the packaging requirement for plush toys?
A: Plush toys like Teddy Bear must be sealed in dust-resistant packaging.

Q: How are warranty claims handled?
A: Warranty requests are reviewed within 5 business days after inspection.
